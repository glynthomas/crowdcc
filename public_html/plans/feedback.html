We want to build crowdcc the way you want

We created this web app to help collect and curate our social media on twitter.  We wanted to be able to quickly scan and copy interesting tweets without having to navigate back forth through our twitter timeline looking for related topics.

We think that other people might find this app useful too.

We are now pondering about other features we could offer users (those using the app for their own use and those who might like to use it as part of a business).

We've already started thinking about the sorts of things you might like and we're keen to have your feedback on those ideas.

We'd also love to hear your suggestions (in the comments section below) for features and enhancements which we haven't thought of (no matter how big or small, from the simple to the more complex).
