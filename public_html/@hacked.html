crowdcc: account recovery instructions

Please read these instructions carefully, shortly you should receive an email (please check your spam folder) and a twitter direct message to your crowdcc registered twitter and email accounts, each message contains a digital key, each key will be required to recover your crowdcc account, as an example, the email key and the twitter digital key will look a bit like the keys copied and pasted into the boxes on this page.

When you receive both your keys press the Next button, then, just like in this example, copy and paste the email key into the email key text box, and the twitter key into the twitter key text box, if any errors are detected (the keys are not in the correct order or are incorrectly copied and pasted), to protect your account, you may need to re-start this account recovery process.
