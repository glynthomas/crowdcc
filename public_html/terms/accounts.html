Accounts

In order for us to continue to offer an innovative social storage service and to fund future development we are planning two paid account options, both will offer increased storage above the free storage limit, but in addition we want to provide addtional features that you really want, here are just a couple of suggestions which we have thought up.

Standard Account : cost $6.00 per month

features : 500cc storage, auto copy your own tweets, auto copy tweets based on one keyword.

Pro Account : cost $12.00 per month

features : 1000cc storage, auto copy your own tweets, auto copy tweets based on two keywords.

Our social storage service is currently only based on API data from twitter, due to customer demand this is subject to change and in the near future other social storage options will be made availiable to paid account customers.

Please visit our blog, and have your say by giving us your feedback. or if you prefer you can contact us directly or reach us on twitter @crowdccHQ.
