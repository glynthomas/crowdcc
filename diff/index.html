share content or carbon copy

For reading later, keeping or deleting. In the timeline view you can simply copy or share the tweet.

As part of this trial phase you'll be able to carbon copy 10 tweets (10cc) you can copy and keep. If you signup by registering your email address with us, we double this and give you an additional 10cc, that's 20cc copy storage, for reading later, read then delete or store.

it's all there, in my timeline and I can store it for as long as I want ...”

Glyn Thomas, founder developer, crowdcc

manage your content

You are in control of what you want to keep and what you no longer want. In the copy view you can re-share the tweet, if the tweet is still active, or delete the tweet from your store.
